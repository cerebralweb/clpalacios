The Outcome

The Face to Face sessions proved to be effective:

  • Confirmed that the majority of the UX worked well
  • Confirmed hypotheses that users would experience some confusion at certain junctures in the overall journey
  • Demonstrated the importance of addressing these issues to the client and design partner
  • Identified a few other issues with the company which would have been difficult to spot with a purely online approach.

The document quoted is: Alex Johnson and Phillip Morrison, "Human Plus Digital Equals Agile and Accurate: How holistic communities can provide depth, rigour and quality to impatient stakeholders", ESOMAR Fusion, 2018
